Ioeuropa

Io and Europa is a educational computer game.

Download .zip Download .tar.gz View on GitHub

Jupiter has two moons that are of very special moons. Io is the first moon we found with volcanic activity, Europa is the first moon we have found that has water on it. What if there has been life here all along, and what else are fire and water supposed to do fight!
